The Seven Wonders of Our World

A safe place built for you, my Anam (Kinderjoy).

1. The First Shock of Joy

Remember how we first met? That day changed the entire trajectory of my life. It was so unexpected, so sudden, but you walked in and instantly replaced the grey with color. You are the best surprise I have ever known.

Memory: "The soft, careful way you spoke, like you were sharing a secret meant just for me. It disarmed the Angry Bird completely."

2. The Weight of My Wounds

I am so profoundly sorry for my actions and for letting my own walls and anxieties affect you. My fear of losing you often pushed you further away, and that is a failure I own entirely. I should have trusted the connection more than I trusted my own panic.

Please forgive me. You deserved unwavering strength, and instead, I gave you uncertainty. I am learning, and I promise to be the man who earns your trust every single day.

3. You Are My Strength

My Kinderjoy, you came into my life with an unexpected softness that instantly disarmed me. Watching you face the lonely hostel and the immense demands of your B. Arch studies, you showed me a strength that inspires me every single day. You are the only person who can truly calm the chaos within me; you are my peace. You saw the good heart beneath the "Angry Bird" and gently helped me find it again, making me a better man.

You are my anchor, Anam. Always.

4. Words for Your Soul

"I love you without knowing how, or when, or from where. I love you simply, without problems or pride." — Pablo Neruda
"When I saw you, I fell in love, and you smiled because you knew." — Arrigo Boito (This is how I felt the first day.)

5. The Commitment That Endures

I made a promise to you that night, and that promise holds true across any distance. I will always be your safe space, your sounding board, and your friend before anything else.

"I don't know if fate will ever allow us to be one together in this world again, but yes, **I love you the most.** You are, and will always be, the one."

With all my love, now and forever,
